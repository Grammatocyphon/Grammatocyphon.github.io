About me

Hello and welcome to my personal web page.
Feel free to look around, discover my interests, and get to know me a little better.

I am a classicist, linguist, and student of ancient history at KU Leuven. My main interests are the Phrygian people and their language, as well as ancient fragmentary languages and sound systems. My curiosity is rarely satiated, however. I have attended several Summer School courses of Languages and Linguistics by Leiden University, and have been getting into mark-up languages, creating relational databases, and my own computer literacy.

In my free time, I like to write poetry in Latin and Dutch. I (should) know how to play the organ, but really only practice on piano. I am also learning to sew.

My native language is Dutch, and I am very comfortable with English. I speak French and German met haar op, but have little difficulty reading. With effort, I can understand Spanish and Italian texts, in particular academic literature. Yet I do not formally know these. Latin and Ancient Greek I know as an object of scholarly study, but I am also somewhat proficient in them. I can read texts with ease, barring the occasional translation error, and with proper thought can compose texts.
